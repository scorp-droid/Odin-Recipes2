Description

Yummy delicious pasta heheheh.

Ingredients

  • 1 box Barilla Penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese to taste, grated

Steps

  1. If using bacon, cook until crisp. Add onion and sauté 2-3 minutes or until translucent.
  2. If not using bacon*, heat skillet with olive oil, add onion and sauté 2-3 minutes or until translucent.
  3. Add tomato sauce, simmer for 10 minutes.
